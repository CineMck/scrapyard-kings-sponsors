About the Show
What Is Scrapyard Kings?
A high-energy competition series where teams build incredible machines from nothing but scrapyard junk — on a ticking clock.
🔧

Pure Build Competition

3 teams dropped into a massive scrapyard with no new parts. They have under 10 hours to design, fabricate, and test machines that can race, float, fly — or all three.

🎬

Cinematic Production

A-list production quality with 4K cameras, drone footage, and Hollywood-level editing. This isn't a webcam in a garage — it's premium entertainment.

💪

Produced with Heavy D

Dave "Heavy D" Sparks (Diesel Brothers, 4M+ YouTube subscribers, 900M+ views) executive produces and hosts. His audience is your audience.
